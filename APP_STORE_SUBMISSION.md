## 7. App Review Information Notes

This is the most important text in the entire submission. Apple reviewers are typically en-US, working on test devices. Anticipate their questions and answer in the notes field.

### Notes for the Reviewer (paste into App Review Information → Notes)

```
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
MISHWARO — REVIEWER NOTES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Thank you for reviewing Mishwaro. To help your review go smoothly, here
is everything you need to know about the app's flows and the regulatory
context.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────
DEMO ACCOUNTS (please use to test the app)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────

PASSENGER demo account:
  Email:    reviewer-passenger@mishwaro.com
  Password: AppleReview2026!

DRIVER demo account (with sample trips already published):
  Email:    reviewer-driver@mishwaro.com
  Password: AppleReview2026!

ADMIN demo account (read-only, to view dashboards):
  Email:    reviewer-admin@mishwaro.com
  Password: AppleReview2026!

The accounts are pre-populated with:
- Sample trips between Ramallah and Nablus (most popular Palestinian
  intercity route)
- One completed trip with a left review, so you can see the review system
- Profile photo + verified driver's license on the driver account
- Sample in-app messages

────────────────────────────────────────────────────
WHAT MISHWARO IS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Mishwaro is a peer-to-peer carpooling app for the Palestinian territories
(West Bank). Drivers post trips they're already taking (e.g. Ramallah →
Nablus, Tuesday 8am, 3 seats available, 25 NIS per seat). Passengers
search and book a seat. Payment to the driver happens OUTSIDE the app —
in cash, via Jawwal Pay (Palestinian mobile money), Reflect, or bank
transfer. The app facilitates discovery, booking confirmation, and
in-app messaging only.

This is the same model as BlaBlaCar (which Apple has approved for many
years) — long-distance carpooling between cities, not on-demand point-
to-point rides.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PAYMENT MODEL — Guideline 3.1.5(a) compliance
────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Mishwaro does NOT use In-App Purchase or any digital goods purchase. It
is fully compliant with App Store Review Guideline 3.1.5(a), which
permits real-world physical transport services to be paid for outside
the app:

  "Apps that enable people to purchase goods or services that will
   be consumed outside of the app must use purchase methods other
   than in-app purchase to collect those payments."

The "service" here is a physical car ride between two real-world
Palestinian cities. The payment goes directly from the passenger to
the driver (the app takes NO percentage and processes NO money). This
is identical to:

  - BlaBlaCar (carpooling, long-distance)
  - Uber (ride-hailing, point-to-point)
  - Lyft (ride-hailing)
  - Cabify (ride-hailing)
  - Yango (Yandex's ride app)

All of which use external payment methods and have been continuously
approved on the App Store.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────
DRIVER VERIFICATION — Guideline 5.1.1(ix) compliance
────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Apple's guideline 5.1.1(ix) applies to apps providing services in
"highly regulated fields including… ridesharing, etc." and requires
verification of the service provider's identity and credentials.

Mishwaro's driver verification process:

1. Mandatory account verification with phone number (OTP via SMS)
2. Mandatory upload of government-issued driver's license photo
3. Mandatory upload of vehicle registration document
4. Mandatory upload of vehicle insurance proof
5. Selfie photo cross-verified against the driver's license photo
6. Manual admin review by Mishwaro staff before driver activation
7. Drivers cannot post trips until status = "verified"

The verification documents are stored in a private storage bucket
(Supabase Storage with row-level-security). Only Mishwaro admins and
the driver themselves can access these documents — passengers see
only the driver's name, profile photo, and aggregate rating.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────
GEOGRAPHIC SCOPE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Mishwaro currently serves the West Bank (Palestinian territories). The
app uses standard ISO country codes and supports Hebrew/Arabic/English
interface text. Cities are referenced by their internationally-recognized
names (e.g. "Jerusalem" = القدس in Arabic, "Ramallah" = رام الله).

The app does not contain any politically sensitive content beyond
listing Palestinian city names. We have reviewed Apple's Guideline 1.1.6
(Discrimination) and 5.4 (VPN/etc.) and confirmed compliance.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PERMISSIONS REQUESTED
────────────────────────────────────────────────────

NSLocationWhenInUseUsageDescription
  → Used when passenger sets pickup point or driver tracks trip progress
  → Only WhenInUse, never Always
  → Permission prompt copy is localized (Arabic for Arabic-system devices,
    English otherwise)

NSCameraUsageDescription
  → Used during driver onboarding (selfie + license photo)
  → Used during passenger verification (optional selfie)
  → Not used for any other purpose

NSPhotoLibraryUsageDescription / NSPhotoLibraryAddUsageDescription
  → Used to choose existing profile photo (alternative to camera)
  → Used to save trip receipts (driver can save earnings record image)

────────────────────────────────────────────────────
THIRD-PARTY SDKs
────────────────────────────────────────────────────

- Supabase (database + auth + storage) — self-hosted infrastructure
  on Supabase Cloud, used for app backend only, no advertising data
- Sentry (error tracking only, anonymous, no PII)

No advertising SDKs. No analytics SDKs that share data with third
parties. No social media SDKs.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────
WHY THE DEMO ACCOUNT MATTERS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────

The app appears empty if a fresh account is created in your test region
because all real trips are between Palestinian cities (no real driver
based in Cupertino). Please use the demo accounts above to see populated
trips, completed bookings, reviews, and the full intended experience.

If anything is unclear, please reach out to me directly:
   souqnamarketplace@gmail.com — I respond within 4 hours.

شكراً لكم — Thank you for your time and review.

— The Mishwaro team
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
```
